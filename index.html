Who We Are

E-Cell FoET is a student-driven initiative working under the Faculty of Engineering & Technology, University of Lucknow. We aim to cultivate entrepreneurial thinking, practical problem solving, and startup readiness among engineering students.

What We Do

From ideation bootcamps and startup talks to hands-on workshops, industry mentorship, and innovation challenges — we bridge the gap between classrooms and the startup ecosystem.

Our Mission

To nurture entrepreneurial talent by providing the right environment, mentorship, and exposure that transforms ideas into scalable real-world ventures.

Our Vision

To establish FoET, University of Lucknow as a recognized hub of innovation and entrepreneurship producing job creators, not just job seekers.

Key Initiatives

Startup Bootcamps

Intensive programs focused on ideation, validation, business models, and pitching.

Founder Talks

Sessions with startup founders, investors, and industry professionals sharing real-world insights.

Innovation Challenges

Hackathons and problem-solving competitions addressing real societal and industrial problems.
