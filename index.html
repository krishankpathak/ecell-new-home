The Entrepreneurship Cell of Faculty of Engineering & Technology, University of Lucknow — empowering students to innovate, build, and lead impactful ventures.

Who We Are

E-Cell FoET is a student-driven initiative under the Faculty of Engineering & Technology, University of Lucknow. We cultivate entrepreneurial mindset, leadership, and problem-solving abilities among students.

What We Do

We organize bootcamps, founder talks, workshops, innovation challenges, and mentorship programs to bridge academics with the startup ecosystem.

Our Mission

To provide the right environment, exposure, and mentorship that helps students convert ideas into scalable ventures.

Our Vision

To establish FoET, University of Lucknow as a recognized entrepreneurship and innovation hub in India.
